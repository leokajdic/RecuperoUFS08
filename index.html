Feline Artworks Exhibition

The first exhibition of the artworks with cats

Our curated collection showcases a diverse range of artistic masterpieces, spanning various periods and artistic movements. Marvel of the meticolous brushstrokes that bring to life the grace and charm of cats in every stroke. From ancient Egyptian depictions that exude a sense of regality to Renaissance portraits that capture the spirit of their feline subjects, each artwork offers a unique glimpse into the timeless fascination with our feline friends
